How to get an account

If you are a scientist in need of data for research, you need to click register and select the scientist option, you will need to give us and email, personal and job information, phone and BI.
If you are a possible donor, and want to join a project and help in research, you need to click register and select the donor option, you will need to give us an email and age, minors are not allowed to participate.

Other Questions

Where to Download App

To download the app you must register and log in as a donor. A download link will show up in the home page.

How to Create/Update Project

To create a project you must be a scientist that's logged in. To create a project all you have to do is go to the navbar and click on "Create a Project". You will then need to insert the information required, like the tipe of sensors, how many times the sensor collects data, etc. To update the project, go to My Projects on the navbar, and click on the specific project you wish to update. You will see an update button on the page and there you can update any parametre.

How to Join a Project

To join a project you mist be logged in as a donor. Simply go to to the Projects tab in the navbar and click List of Projects. You will then see a list of every project, you can then pick and select which you want to join.
