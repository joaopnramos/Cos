Frequently Asked Questions

Who we are

Basic Info

We are a informatic engineering student duo who want to make a difference and develop applications based on security and efficiency.

We started with a simple premise: phone sensor data collected and converted into information that can be used for research pourposes.

Accounts

How to get an account

If you are a scientist in need of data for research, you need to click register and select the scientist option, you will need to give us an email, personal and job information, phone and CC.

If you are a possible donator, and want to join a project and help in research, you need to click register and select the donor option, you will need to give us an email and age, minors are not allowed to participate.

Donor

Donors welcome! Without you this project is nothing. Everything done in this program requires your sensor data, you will be helping scientists with research from all aspects of life, join us and start today!

Scientist

Scientists welcome! This is the place for you, if you've ever wanted to collect data specific from phone sensors for your research, this is the website for you.

Other Questions

Where to Download App

To download the app you must register and log in as a donor. A download link will show up in the home page.

How to Create/Update Project

To create a project you must be a scientist that's logged in. To create a project all you have to do is go to the navbar and click on "Create a Project".

You will then need to insert the information required, like the tipe of sensors, how many times the sensor collects data, etc.

To update the project, go to My Projects on the navbar, and click on the specific project you wish to update. You will see an update button on the page and there you can update any parametre.

How to Join a Project

To join a project you mist be logged in as a donor. Simply go to to the Projects tab in the navbar and click List of Projects.

You will then see a list of every project, you can then pick and select which you want to join.
