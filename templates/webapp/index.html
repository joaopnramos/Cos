Who We Are


OBJECTIVE: We are a computer engineering duo from Portugal who wanted to create an app that allows science researchers to use data collected from everyday's people devices for research.


HOW WE'RE DOING IT: Using a combination of knowledge we learned in the three years of our bachelor degree, some Django, Bootstrap, and Android Studio, we were able to not only create a functional website, but also an application for android devices to collect said data for research.
